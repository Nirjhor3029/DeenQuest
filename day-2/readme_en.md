## 🧠 Knowledge: What is a Blessing?

A blessing (ni'mah) is a gift from Allah 🎁

Things we use every day but often forget about are Allah's blessings.

### Examples:
| 👀 Eyes | 👂 Ears | 👃 Nose | ❤️ Heart |
|--------|---------|---------|----------|
| 🍚 Food | 💧 Water | 👨‍👩‍👧 Family | 🌙 Moon |

**We did not create these ourselves — Allah gave them with mercy.**

---

## 👀 The Amazing Gift of Eyes

Imagine if you had **no eyes**:
- Could not read books 📚
- Could not see your parents 👨‍👩‍👧
- Could not enjoy the moon 🌙
- Could not know colors 🌈

**So eyes are not just body parts — they are an amazing gift from Allah! ✨**

---

## 💧 Water & Food: Life Supporters

- No water = No life 💧
- No food = No energy 🍚
- Without rain = No food 🌧️🌾

> **Deep lesson:** We depend on Allah every single day!

## 🧠 Memorization: Alhmadullilah

**Arabic:**
```
ٱلْحَمْدُ لِلَّٰهِ
```

**Pronunciation:**
> Alhmadullilah

**Meaning:**
All praise belongs to Allah

---

## 💎 Deep Knowledge Corner

When we say Alhmadullilah, we do 3 things:
1. ✅ Recognize blessings
2. ✅ Thank Allah
3. ❤️ Increase gratitude in heart

This decreases pride and increases Iman!
