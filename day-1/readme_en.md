## 🧠 1) Who is Allah?

**Answer:**

Allah is our Creator.

He created the sky, earth, sun, moon, stars, mountains, rivers, trees, birds, and humans.

Nothing can create itself. Just like a toy car cannot build itself, someone must make it.

In the same way, this whole universe has a Creator. That Creator is Allah.

---

## ❤️ 2) Why did Allah create us?

Allah created us:

- To know Him
- To love Him
- To obey Him
- To become good people
- To earn Paradise (Jannah)

**Simply:** Allah wants us to be honest, kind, and good humans.

---

## 👀 3) Allah always sees us

Even if you are alone, Allah sees you.
He knows when you do good deeds and when you make mistakes.

This is not to scare you.
It reminds us that Allah is always aware of us and cares for us.
